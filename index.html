About

To me, success is not about money - It's about what I design.

Kaelpi Design is a small Graphic and web design studio, commited to provide top quality designs and excellent service to our clients. From branding, web design, and illustration; our diverse services allow a single point of contact for all your design needs.

Working alongside start-ups, entrepreneurs or small business owners and under the creative direction of Marla Jean Tarrant, Kaelpi Design creates emotionally driven designs with a focus on blurring the lines between art, design and functionality.

We build close relationships with our clients, priding ourselves on offering the very best customer service with honesty and reliability as valued in our studio as creativity and innovation.
