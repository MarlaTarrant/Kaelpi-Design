About

To me, success is not about money - It's about what I design.

Before graduating from college with a degree in Graphic Design, I found a passion for design when I started sketching during and after school, and knew I had to pursue a career in that field.

After years of working full time, while freelancing as a Graphic Designer, I decided it was time to start my own Graphic Design studio where I would be working alongside start-ups, entrepreneurs or small business owners, to create emotionally driven designs with a focus on blurring the lines between art, design and functionality.

My mission is to provide top quality designs and excellent service to all clients. From branding, web design, and illustration; our diverse services allow a single point of contact for all your design needs.

Where does 'Kaelpi Design' come from?
Kelpie, or water Kelpie, is the Scots name given to a shape-shifting water spirit inhabiting the lochs and pools of Scotland. I combined my love for horses with my ancestral origin, tweaked the name a bit, and so, Kaelpi Design was born.
