Zane & Bernice Wedding invitation
Bespoke save the date invitation for their special day.

Description

  • Client: Bernice
  • Date: 2018

It was a great pleasure working with Bernice and her fiance, Zane, on this project. Her wedding theme is natural-like, which included pastel colors and lots of greenery. She wanted a simple and classy 'save the date' that would enable users to RSVP on the website by clicking on the invitation.

We incorporated the forestry theme into the 'save the date' with a fairy lit forest in the background, as the wedding was held at a beautiful venue in the forest.
